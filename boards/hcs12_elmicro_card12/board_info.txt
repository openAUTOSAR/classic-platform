
Elmicro Card12 HC12 Controller Module with MC9S12DP512

  http://elmicro.com/en/card12.html

MC9S12DP512 Microcontroller Info:
  MC9S12DP512 MCU with LQFP112 package
  512 KB Flash
  4 KB EEPROM
  14 KB RAM
  3x SPI
  2x SCI
  5x CAN
  Enhanced Capture Timer
  8 Channel PWM
  16 Channel 10 Bit A/D-Converter
  
  
Board Features:
  16 MHz Quarz Clock (8 MHz System Clock)
  RS232 Ports with Transceiver MAX232A
  PCA82C251 CAN Physical Interface
  Special Reset Controller
  Reset Switch
  LED
  BDM (Background Debug Mode) connector
  All controller pins are available at two double row headers
  5V power supply
